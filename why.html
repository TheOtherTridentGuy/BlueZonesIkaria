Diet:

  • Emphasis on local and homegrown vegetables, legumes, and greens. Just about everything they eat is home-cooked, so their meals are less processed, and contain no preservatives or chemicals.
  • Their diet is rich in freshly caught seafood. It also includes a lot of olives and olive oil.
  • Local wine is also consumed regularly.
